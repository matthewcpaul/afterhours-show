01

Opening Reception

November 4, 2015, 6:00pm – 10:00pm
Lewis Carnegie

Afterhours is a poster party off the clock. It's part individual creative expression, part curated poster show, and part non-profit fundraiser. 30 artists, 30 posters, 30 bucks.

All the prints will be sold over the course of the show with a portion of the proceeds to the artists, and a portion going to this year’s non-profit, The Capital Area Food Bank of Texas.
